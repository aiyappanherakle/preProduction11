GreatCollections has been built to serve the numismatic community to offer an extensive selection of certified coins for auction and direct sale, without the risks of dealing with unknown people or companies online.

GreatCollections has physical possession of every coin being offered for sale (by fixed price or auction), which is additional assurance that you are bidding on the actual coin you will receive.

Other advantages when dealing with GreatCollections:

Consistent professional photography:
- Our in-house professional photographers image each item consistently and without image enhancement.

Confidential transactions:
- All transactions are confidential between buyer and seller; your personal information as a buyer or seller is only disclosed to GreatCollections.
