Todd McKenna - Numismatist / Consignment Director
Todd began his career in numismatics in 2011, focusing on world coins, while employed by a leading world coin auction house. In particular, Todd researched machine-struck Chinese coins, British coinage of nearly all types and Crown-sized coins of Europe. At GreatCollections, Todd researches coin values and appraises U.S. and Foreign coins for collectors and dealers, values estates for law firms and accountants, processes uncertified coins for grading and works with consignors directly throughout the consignment process. He is always available to share his knowledge about numismatics.

Todd is Southern California native and in his spare time enjoys attending comic conventions to expand his collection of 1960s sci-fi and horror comics as well as playing large-format board game with friends.
